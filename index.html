  • ExpendiPal

    ExpendiPal is an expense tracking system that allows you to scan receipts and provides advice on your spending habits, highlighting where you're doing well and where there's room for improvement. It aims to resolve a prevalent issue found in current receipt scanning applications. Existing systems often fail to categorize each item in a purchase accurately, leading to less precise spending insights. By meticulously tracking each item, ExpendiPal ensures accurate monitoring of user spending habits and provides more insightful feedback.
